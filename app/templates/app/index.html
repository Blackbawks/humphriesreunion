Directions!

Getting here is easy!

When you land in St. John's, head to the outer ring road by driving south on Torbay road. Once there, head West on the Trans Canada Highway.... keep heading west... don't stop heading west... (you're actually going NorthWest).. Take the turnoff for Gambo (about 3 hours outside of st. john's)... about an hour along that road, you'll get to the turn off for the valleyfield section of New Wes Valley!
